Research Notebooks
==================

CaVi_4bin_pipeline.ipynb
    Unified four-bin research pipeline and thesis analysis for COL_PH, SUN,
    EVE, and MID.

[V4] DAV2.ipynb
    Depth Anything V2 development and evaluation notebook.

[V5] IS+FPN.ipynb
    Instance-segmentation model development and evaluation notebook.

[V5] Zero-3DCE.ipynb
    Zero-3DCE enhancement model development and evaluation notebook.

These notebooks are intended for Google Colab. Before running one, mount the
required Google Drive location and verify that its input paths point to the
corresponding folders in this package. The notebooks may require a GPU and
their Python dependencies are defined within their cells.

The notebooks contain research and training workflows. The precomputed web
demo does not require them to run.
